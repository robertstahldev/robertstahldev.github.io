Values & Ethics

Throughout my 21 years in the military, I developed a firm approach to teamwork and responsibility. I led teams, managed critical projects, and made decisions when direction wasn't clear. Just as importantly, I learned how to listen and grow. I value feedback, stay open to coaching, and support the mission from any position.

I understand that trust isn't inherent, it's earned. I build it through consistency and thoughtful communication. Whether I'm leading an effort or contributing as part of the team, I maintain my focus: improve, add value, and help the group succeed.
